1. `research_statement.md` - High-level synthesis of the lab's mission, questions, hypotheses, methods, canon, and open problems.
2. `ideation.md` - Place to write down experiment ideas by noticing a gap, checking prior work, and forming a hypothesis.
3. `experiments.md` - Place to design experiments and define what evidence would be convincing.

5. `raw/` - Archive of source materials including papers, articles, images, and the Neurogenesis code repo.
6. `raw/index.md` - Minimal map of the archived source materials in `raw/`.
7. `summaries/` - Distilled notes and summaries derived from the raw materials.
8. `AGENTS.md` - Basic operating instructions for agents working in this knowledge base.
