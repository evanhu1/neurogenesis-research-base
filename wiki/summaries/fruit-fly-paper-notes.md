# Fruit Fly Brain Paper Notes

## Summary

- Source type: freeform research notes rather than a formal paper.
- Dominant theme: biological neural function is robust to large underlying
  parameter variation; similar behavior can arise from very different parameter
  settings.
- Main mechanistic motifs collected:
- Disinhibition.
- Feedforward inhibition.
- Negative feedback.
- Recurrent excitation.
- Varying inhibitory/excitatory ratios.
- Structural theme: emphasizes neuron order and multi-stage sensory-to-motor
  processing rather than simple reflex arcs.
- Developmental theme: argues that genomes encode generative wiring rules, not
  explicit synapse-by-synapse blueprints.
- Key developmental ingredients named in the notes: morphogen gradients,
  neurogenesis schedules, axon guidance, synaptogenesis, selective cell death,
  and activity-dependent pruning.
- Broader takeaway: robustness, recurrence, homeostasis, and compressed
  developmental programs are central features of biological nervous systems.
